Introduction to Machine Learning

The objective of this introductory module is to provide the students with an understanding of the concept of Machine Learning (ML) and explore it's vital role in our past, present and future society. Machine Learning has evolved to support a wide range of everyday applications, from digital experiences to automated decision-making in sectors such as finance, healthcare, and logistics. The section focuses at the way real-time big data improves autonomous decision-making and permits adaptive learning procedures.

Learning Outcomes

  • • Have a better understanding of the of role machine learning in future industry.
  • • Identify skill sets required to become proficient in machine learning.
  • • Know about the pitfalls of machine learning and ways to address it.
